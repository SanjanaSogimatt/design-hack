Water

Water energy shows up mainly in two different ways. Water can be still and deep, like a lake or pond, or it can be dynamic and flowing, like a river. The still aspect of water points to its connection to wisdom, intuition, and introspection. The more dynamic side of water is related to how you interact with the world
